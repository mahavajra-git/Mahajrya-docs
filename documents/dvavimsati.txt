<h1>Organization Charter: Dvavimsati</h1>

<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>This introductory chapter states that the organization studies twenty-two guiding capabilities and adapts them to practical governance, documentation, and accountability.</p>

<h2>Anima</h2>
<p>This chapter outlines the ability to perceive subtle details and mandates reviews for the smallest operational decisions.</p>

<h2>Mahima</h2>
<p>This chapter elevates long-term vision, requiring leadership to assess how every action scales to the largest possible consequence.</p>

<h2>Garima</h2>
<p>This chapter codifies steadiness and gravity, assigning custodianship roles to maintain continuity under pressure.</p>

<h2>Laghima</h2>
<p>This chapter introduces agility policies so teams can shed unnecessary process and move lighter when urgency demands.</p>

<h2>Prapti</h2>
<p>This chapter defines reach and access, clarifying authority for securing resources across jurisdictions.</p>

<h2>Prakamya</h2>
<p>This chapter records the power of fulfillment, describing how directives become enacted and verified.</p>

<h2>Ishitva</h2>
<p>This chapter specifies sovereignty protocols that limit unilateral control while preserving decisive action.</p>

<h2>Vashitva</h2>
<p>This chapter establishes influence guidelines, ensuring persuasive power is transparent and accountable.</p>

<h2>Kamarutatva</h2>
<p>This chapter protects freedom of purposeful desire, documenting how new initiatives receive charter approval.</p>

<h2>Anumimatvam</h2>
<p>This chapter describes consent-based authority, detailing the approvals required before invoking exceptional powers.</p>

<h2>Sravana Darshanam</h2>
<p>This chapter unites listening and observation standards, mandating record-keeping for hearings and audits.</p>

<h2>Manah Javah</h2>
<p>This chapter emphasizes speed of mind, providing procedures for rapid analysis without sacrificing diligence.</p>

<h2>Trikala</h2>
<p>This chapter encodes past, present, and future review cycles for all strategic plans.</p>

<h2>Samartha</h2>
<p>This chapter documents capability benchmarks so teams can demonstrate competency before assuming mandates.</p>

<h2>Vishwabal</h2>
<p>This chapter asserts global strength, defining cross-border coordination clauses.</p>

<h2>Avalokitesh</h2>
<p>This chapter preserves compassionate oversight, describing ombud processes for grievances.</p>

<h2>Pravacana</h2>
<p>This chapter ensures clear proclamation, standardizing how rulings and updates are published.</p>

<h2>Karmika</h2>
<p>This chapter regulates actionable labor, specifying ethical execution of directives.</p>

<h2>Buddhana</h2>
<p>This chapter encourages awakened discernment, requiring reflective assessments before major decisions.</p>

<h2>Nirmarupa</h2>
<p>This chapter defines formless adaptability, guiding how structures may transform without losing purpose.</p>

<h2>Sadanavina</h2>
<p>This chapter safeguards perpetual renewal, mandating continuous improvement cycles.</p>

<h2>Mahajrya</h2>
<p>This chapter concludes with a statement on great conduct, reaffirming that every chapter remains amendable when clarity or justice demands.</p>
